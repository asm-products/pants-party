Beliefs

We believe that funny is funny. We believe that not funny belongs on CBS. We believe that no matter how clever you are, if you can't find a good dick joke funny, you're not our kind of person. We believe that even if a joke offends people, it can still be funny, and also that jokes don't have to be ha-ha funny to be funny. We believe that people falling down is as funny as razor sharp satire, and that sometimes food words — like sauerkraut or kielbasa — are inherently funny.

Sometimes, we're going to cross the line, and we believe that's okay.

Sometimes, you're going to cross the line, and we believe that's okay.

We believe our community is only going to be as strong and as funny as our members, and we want our members to be all kinds of people. There's no barrier to entry here, no bouncer at the door of the Pants.Party. So get your ass in here and grab a beer.

Because we believe you'll need more than a few to stand spending time with us.
