About Pants Party

You know how everything in the new internet economy has to be collaborative? And how marketing managers and new-school educators alike are touting collaboration as the next great thing in internet culture as if people haven't been collaborating on things for thousands of fucking years?

We like that shit too, which is why we built Pants.Party.

Imagine Pants.Party as a game of hot potato. Whoever has the potato is telling the joke. And say I start the joke, and I say, "You ever hear the one about the two Irishmen and the mermaid?" and then I toss the potato to you. Now that you have the potato, you have to finish the joke.

Some of us have intestinal worms inside of us, but all of us have a funny bone or two inside of us. We think you're full of punchlines and you don't even know it, and we want you to come to the Pants.Party and show us what you've got.

I mean, other than abject failure connected to a screenname via which we can probably locate you IRL and mock you to your face in front of your love interest, what's the worst that could happen?
