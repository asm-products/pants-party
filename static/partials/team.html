Team

Pants.Party's team is just like that team that you liked in that movie that time — a rag-tag group of ne'er-do-wells who somehow triumph against all odds. Or something like that. We're kind of like the kids in The Goonies and the kids in The Wizard except grown up. So, like, dorks that can't talk to girls.

Still, we seem to get along, and make stuff, and now you're here hanging out with us, so we can't be that bad.

Here's who we are in a bit more detail:

Barry: One of the co-creators of Pants.Party, Barry was born with an overactive thyroid, causing him to grow to gargantuan size before his twelfth birthday. As a result, his childhood name was Hulk. Unlike the Hulk, however, his size wasn't due to muscles, or or muscle mass. Like the Hulk, Barry was and still is green in color. Doctors don't know what's up with that, and they gave up on him a while back. For obvious reasons, Barry is single.

Scot: The other co-creator of Pants.Party, Scot is an illustrator, a designer, and an artist who took a wrong turn somewhere and got involved with Barry. He regrets this daily, but because they share a terrible secret, he can't leave Barry's side for long. If you like the way Pants.Party looks, it's because of Scot.

Kaveh: Kaveh is the next most important person at Pants.Party. He makes things and fixes things. Put him in front of a keyboard and he's Mozart to everyone else's Salieri. Put him in front of a four-burner stovetop and he's a Mozart to everyone else's Salieri — though it should be mentioned that Mozart was a terrible cook. Kaveh's favourite jokes are strange. They start out as knock-knock jokes and end up as dirty limericks.

Dave: If you like the way things sound on Pants.Party, that's Dave. He's a writer, and has been his whole life because he thought it would get him paid and laid. He was half right. It got him a bunch of shirts, all of which are plaid.
